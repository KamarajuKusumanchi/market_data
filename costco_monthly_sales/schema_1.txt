Costco publishes the net sales for every month.

This data is stored in the following columns

for: YYYY-MM
report_date: YYYY-MM-DD
net_sale: billions of dollars

To update the data:
* Go to https://investor.costco.com/news/default.aspx
* select the year
* Look for links such as "Costco Wholesale Corporation Reports December Sales Results"
